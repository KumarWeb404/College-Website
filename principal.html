RIET Campus - Institute of Engineering & Technology

Welcome from the Principal

You are indeed blessed to find opportunity of being educated at one of the best Institution of Punjab, Ramgarhia Institute of Engg & Tech, that has been nurtured and benedicted by the great Ramgarhia community's divine and celestial vision. This is my firm conviction that you would carry the rich values and traditions imbibed here to greater heights. Our institution has the privilege to have healthy and harmonious ambience and the credit goes to the blessing of our elite institution's management and the diligence put in by learned teachers. This institution has played a cascading role in the carrier and personality of innumerable students who have brought laurels to the college, state, and country alike. I intently believe that you would have an all-round development of your personality during your stay in this exhilarating temple of learning. I wish the best for all our students, and the members of the institution who reiterate their aims at providing the best in academic and extra-curricular fields. Dr. Naveen Dhillon
Principal

The Heart and Soul of the Inspired vision of Change.

The Institute endeavours to impart quality education and provide holistic development to the students. Its primary focus is to fine tune the education delivery systems and internal processes in accordance with the needs and challenges of the comtemporary world.
