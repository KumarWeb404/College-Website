Campus Leadership

Ramgarhia Educational Council, Phagwara is managing more than a dozen schools and colleges since 1929. The Council is whole heartedly committed to promote excellence and service through its untiring efforts to grow continuously.

Presently, Ramgarhia Educational Institutions are marching ahead to serve the society with quality education in different fields under the dynamic & able guidance of young, farsighted & energetic President & Chairperson Mrs. Manpreet Kaur Bhogal .
